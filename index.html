Game Info

"Knights vs. Golem" is a simple jump and ran game. You have to collect swords and magic bottles with your knight while keeping an eye out for your enemies. You can destroy the enemies by jumping on them. In the end, you'll face the final boss, whom you can defeat by throwing the collected swords at them.
